Chatbots are representatives of the company

Unlike existing approaches, chatbots are not limited by a pre-planned dialogue route, but naturally respond to co-developers' cues.

They are proactive and use dialogues and specific skills to fulfill their role task. They know how to work in any popular messenger, on marketplaces and social networks, creating a single point for communication.

Seller

Answers questions about the company and products/services, recommends and forms orders. The difference from standard chatbots in the approach: tries to find out the internal needs of the buyer in a dialogue and make a value proposition, selling as a junior salesperson, but constantly learning.

Support

BizAI takes over all tasks of the first line of support. It can answer questions according to the context of your company, accept requests and send notifications about status changes.

Recruiter

Helps recruiters quickly and efficiently process applications from candidates and provides convenient communication with them. BizAI pre-screens candidates by analyzing their profile and CV and quickly selects the best candidates for further consideration.

Purchasing

Ensures communication with suppliers, both existing and potential. Accepts proposals so that they do not end up in spam, informs about needs, ensures document flow.

For investors

Responsible for communicating with investors, both current and prospective. Provides them with information on request with different levels of access, immediately performing the function of an analyst working with a "virtual data room". Also periodically informs investors about events in the life of the company.
